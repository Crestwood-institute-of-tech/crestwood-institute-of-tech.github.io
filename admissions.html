Admissions at CIT

Your journey to a rewarding tech career starts here. Follow our simple steps to apply!

How to Apply

Our streamlined application process makes it easy for you to join the CIT family.

1

Choose Your Program

Explore our Programs & Courses section to find the perfect fit for your career aspirations.

2

Review Requirements

Check the specific admission criteria for your chosen program on its dedicated page.

3

Complete Online Form

Fill out our comprehensive online application form below with all necessary details.

4

Pay Application Fee

A non-refundable application fee of UGX 50,000 is required to process your application.

5

Await Decision

Our admissions team will review your application and notify you of the outcome within 2-3 business days.

Admission Requirements

General requirements apply to all programs, with specific criteria for each. Please refer to the individual program pages for detailed requirements.

  • Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) with at least 5 passes (or equivalent).
  • Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) with at least 2 principal passes (or equivalent, for Diploma programs).
  • Basic computer literacy and internet navigation skills.
  • Access to a reliable computer and stable internet connection for online/blended learning.
  • Valid identification (National ID, Passport).

Note: Some programs may have additional prerequisites or entry exams. Check your desired program's page for specifics.

Entry Paths

CIT offers various entry routes to accommodate diverse academic backgrounds and professional experiences.

Direct Entry

For students who have completed secondary education (UCE/UACE or equivalent) and meet program-specific requirements.

Mature Entry

Applicants aged 25 years and above with relevant work experience but without standard academic qualifications may apply.

Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL)

Experienced professionals can apply to have their existing skills and knowledge formally recognized for course credit.
