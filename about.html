About Crestwood Institute of Technology

Learn about our mission, values, and the journey to becoming a leader in tech education.

Our Journey: Engineering Excellence

Crestwood Institute of Technology (CIT) was founded in 2023 with a bold vision: to bridge the digital skills gap in Uganda and beyond. Recognizing the rapid evolution of technology, our founders, a team of passionate educators and tech industry veterans, envisioned an institution that would offer cutting-edge, industry-relevant training accessible to all. Starting as a fully online and blended learning platform, CIT is quickly establishing itself as a beacon of innovation in tech education.

Our commitment lies in providing practical, hands-on learning experiences that equip students with the skills demanded by today’s dynamic tech landscape. We believe in fostering a culture of continuous learning, critical thinking, and problem-solving, preparing our graduates not just for jobs, but for impactful careers that will shape the future.

Core Values

  • Innovation: Embracing new technologies and teaching methodologies.
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in education and outcomes.

  • Community: Fostering collaboration and positive impact.

Our Strategic Objectives

Our roadmap for growth, innovation, and impact in the tech education landscape.

  • To expand our program offerings to meet evolving industry demands.
  • To establish strong partnerships with leading tech companies for internships and job placements.
  • To develop a state-of-the-art physical campus to complement our online learning.
  • To enhance research and development initiatives in emerging technologies.
  • To ensure every graduate is equipped with practical skills and a global mindset.
