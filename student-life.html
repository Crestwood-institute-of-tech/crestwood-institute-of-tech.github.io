Student Life at CIT

Beyond academics, CIT offers a vibrant and supportive environment for personal growth and community engagement.

Clubs & Societies

Join a diverse range of student clubs and societies to pursue your passions, develop new skills, and connect with like-minded individuals.

Coding Club

Weekly hackathons, coding challenges, and collaborative projects.

Cybersecurity Guild

Ethical hacking, CTF competitions, and security awareness campaigns.

Data Science Society

Workshops on data analysis, machine learning, and visualization.

Robotics & AI Club

Hands-on robotics projects, AI model development, and innovation challenges.

Digital Marketing Crew

Learn and practice SEO, social media, and content marketing strategies.

Community Outreach Club

Leverage tech skills for social impact projects in local communities.

Interested in starting a new club? Contact the Student Affairs Office!

Hostel Life (Future Physical Campus)

For our future physical campus, we envision comfortable and secure hostel accommodations, fostering a supportive and engaging living environment for students.

Comfortable Rooms

Well-furnished rooms with study areas and internet access.

Dining Facilities

Nutritious meals provided in a communal dining hall.

High-Speed Internet

Reliable internet access for academic and personal use.

24/7 Security

Ensuring a safe and secure living environment for all residents.

Recreational Areas

Common rooms, sports facilities, and green spaces for relaxation.

More details on hostel application and fees will be available closer to the physical campus launch.

Counseling & Support Services

CIT is committed to the holistic well-being of our students. We offer a range of support services to ensure you thrive academically and personally.

Academic Advising

Guidance on course selection, academic planning, and study strategies.

Personal Counseling

Confidential support for mental health, stress management, and personal challenges.

Peer Mentorship

Connect with experienced students for advice and support.

Disability Support

Accommodations and resources for students with disabilities.

To schedule an appointment or learn more, please contact our Student Support Coordinator.
