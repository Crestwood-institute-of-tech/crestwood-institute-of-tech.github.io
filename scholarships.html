Scholarships & Financial Aid

CIT is committed to making quality tech education accessible to all deserving students.

Overview of Opportunities

We offer a range of scholarships and financial aid options to support our students on their academic journey. Explore the categories below to find what suits you.

Merit-Based Scholarships

Awarded to high-achieving students who demonstrate exceptional academic performance and potential in technology-related fields. These scholarships recognize excellence and encourage continued academic rigor.

Eligibility:
  • Minimum aggregate of [X] points in UACE or equivalent.
  • Strong academic records in STEM subjects.
  • Demonstrated leadership potential or participation in tech-related extracurriculars.
How to Apply

Need-Based Financial Aid

Designed to support talented students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds. Our aim is to remove financial barriers and ensure access to quality tech education.

Eligibility:
  • Demonstrable financial need (requires submission of financial statements/proof of income).
  • Good academic standing (minimum [Y] aggregate in UCE/UACE).
  • Ugandan citizen residing in Uganda.
How to Apply

Women in Tech Scholarships

An initiative to empower and encourage more women to pursue careers in technology. These scholarships aim to increase female representation in the tech industry.

Eligibility:
  • Female Ugandan citizen.
  • Enrolled or accepted into a CIT tech program.
  • Passion for technology and a commitment to making an impact.
How to Apply

Community Impact Scholarships

For students dedicated to leveraging technology for community development and social good. Preference given to those with innovative project ideas.

Eligibility:
  • Demonstrated commitment to community service.
  • Proposal for a tech-driven community project.
  • Good academic standing.
How to Apply

How to Apply for Scholarships

Follow these steps to submit a successful scholarship application:

1

Review Eligibility

Carefully read the eligibility criteria for each scholarship you are interested in.

2

Prepare Documents

Gather all required documents: academic transcripts, recommendation letters, personal statement, financial proof (if applicable).

3

Complete Application

Fill out the online scholarship application form completely and accurately.

4

Submit by Deadline

Ensure your application and all supporting documents are submitted before the specified deadline.

5

Interview (if required)

Shortlisted candidates may be invited for an interview or assessment.
