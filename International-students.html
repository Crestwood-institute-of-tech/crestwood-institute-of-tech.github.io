International Students

Welcome to CIT! We embrace diversity and welcome students from across the globe.

Why Study in Uganda?

Uganda, the "Pearl of Africa," offers a unique blend of vibrant culture, natural beauty, and a growing tech ecosystem. It's an ideal place for international students seeking a rich academic and life experience.

Diverse Culture

Experience a warm and welcoming society with rich cultural traditions.

Natural Beauty

Explore stunning landscapes, wildlife, and national parks during your studies.

Growing Tech Hub

Be part of an emerging tech scene with increasing innovation and opportunities.

Affordable Living

Enjoy a high quality of life with relatively lower living costs compared to many other study destinations.

International Admission Process

Applying to CIT as an international student involves a few additional steps. Our admissions team is here to assist you.

1

Choose Program & Apply

Select your desired program and complete the online application form, ensuring all academic documents are translated to English.

2

Academic Equivalence

Your qualifications will be assessed for equivalence to Ugandan academic standards. We may require external verification.

3

Receive Provisional Offer

If successful, you will receive a provisional admission letter from CIT.

4

Visa Application

Use your provisional admission letter to apply for a student visa at the nearest Ugandan Embassy/High Commission.

5

Final Admission & Enrollment

Upon visa approval and arrival, complete your enrollment and begin your studies!
